General Awareness played an important role in the IBPS RRB PO Mains 2025 exam conducted on 28 December 2025. This section mainly checks how well candidates are aware of recent events, banking updates, and rural economy related topics. The questions are mostly direct, but they need regular reading and smart revision. Below is a clear and simple overview of the GA questions asked in the IBPS RRB PO Mains Exam 2025.
GA Questions Asked In IBPS RRB PO Mains 2025
The GA section in RRB PO Mains 2025 focused more on current affairs of the last 6–8 months, especially topics linked to banking, government schemes, and the economy. Static GK was asked, but mostly mixed with current news. Most questions were one-liners, fact-based, and related to recent events. Candidates who followed daily news and monthly current affairs found this section scoring.

IBPS RRB PO Mains Exam 2025 GA Difficulty Level
The difficulty level of the GA Questions asked in IBPS RRB PO Mains 2025 was Moderate. According to candidate feedback, the questions were direct and mostly based on recent current affairs, banking awareness, and government schemes, with very few confusing questions. Aspirants who followed daily news and revised the last 6–8 months of current affairs were able to attempt a good number of questions with confidence, making General Awareness a scoring section in the exam.

Tips To Prepare For GA in Upcoming Banking Exams
Read current affairs daily, with major focus on banking, economy, and government schemes
Read at least the last 6–8 months of current affairs before the exam
Give extra time to banking and financial awareness topics like RBI updates, rates, and terms
Make short notes for schemes, appointments, and important reports for quick revision
Revise important days, themes, and static GK linked to recent news
Practice GA questions from previous banking exams to understand the pattern
Revise regularly instead of studying everything at the last moment
