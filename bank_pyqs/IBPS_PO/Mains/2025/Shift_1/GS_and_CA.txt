GA Questions Asked in IBPS PO Mains 2025 - 12th October 2025
1. According to the recent updates, C.P. Radhakrishnan served as the Governor of which of the following states?
Answer:
Jharkhand
Explanation:
As of September 2025, C.P. Radhakrishnan is no longer a reviewing governor. He resigned from his gubernatorial post in Maharashtra and was sworn in as the 15th Vice President of India on September 12, 2025.
Before his election as Vice President, Radhakrishnan held several gubernatorial positions, including:
Governor of Maharashtra (July 2024 – September 2025)
Governor of Jharkhand (February 2023 – July 2024)
Additional Charge as Governor of Telangana (March 2024 – July 2024)
Additional Charge as Lieutenant Governor of Puducherry (March 2024 – August 2024)
2. The Vibrant Village Programme – II has been implemented by which Indian state?
Answer:
Arunachal Pradesh
3. The "Bhavishya" scheme is an online pension sanction and payment tracking system created for central government employees in
India
who are about to __________ (Target group).
Answer:
Retiring employees
4. The Gulf of Mannar Marine National Park, located along the Tamil Nadu coast, comprises how many islands?
Answer:
21
5. In the Swachh Vayu Survekshan 2025, which city did not rank among the top three in Category I (population over 10 lakh)?
Answer:
In the Swachh Vayu Survekshan 2025, the top three cities in Category I (population over 10 lakh) were:
Indore – 1st place
Jabalpur – 2nd place
Agra and Surat – tied for 3rd place
