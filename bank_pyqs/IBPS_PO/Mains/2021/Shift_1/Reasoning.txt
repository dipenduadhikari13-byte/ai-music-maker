Arguments: 
I. Yes. This will considerably simplify the tax collection mechanism and the cost of collecting tax will also 
reduce. 
II. Yes. The manufacturers and traders will be benefited by this which in turn will boost tax collection. 
III. No. No other country has adopted such system. 
(a) None is strong 
(b) Only I and III are strong 
(c) Only II is strong 
(d) Only II and III are strong 
(e) None of these

III. Yes. Employees get their illegal demands fulfilled through these unions. 
IV. No. Trade unions are not banned in other economically advanced countries. 
(a) Only I is strong 
(b) Only II is strong 
(c) Only I and II are strong
