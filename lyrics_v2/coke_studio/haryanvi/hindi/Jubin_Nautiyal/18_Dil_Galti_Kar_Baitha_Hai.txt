Title:  Dil Galti Kar Baitha Hai
Artist: Jubin Nautiyal
──────────────────────────────────────────────────

Dil Galti
Mukhda
Ye humne soch rakha thaa, mohabbat na karenge hum..
Kisi ko dil nahin denge, kisi pe na marenge hum..
Tumhari mast aankhon ne ye kaisa haal kar daalaa
Ki ye dil cheez kya hai maang lo to jaan denge hum
Palat ke ishq ki galiyon se jaana hai bada mushhkil
Dil ghalti kar baitha hai, ghalti kar baitha hai dil.
Antara
Khamoshiyaan sunte
kab tak rahenge hum
Mauka hai honthon se, aaj kuchh to kaho
Badi mukhtasar si hai..
Ye zindagi yaara..
Zara paas aa jaao, sochte na raho..
Tum apna lo ya thukraa do
Tumhein haq faisle ka hai..
Humein kya poochhte ho tum
Humare haath me kya hai
Ki hum to kar chuke jaanaa tumehin har saans me shamil
Dil ghalti kar baitha hai..
Koda
Ki pahle-pahle ho gayi humse deedar ki ghalti
Dobara kar gaye hum be-iradaa pyar ki ghalti
Humein zid thee tumhare ishq me hum katl ho jayein
To tumpe mar mita ye dil, aakhiri baar ki ghalti.
Kahan ashik koi hum sa kahan tum sa koi katil?
Dil ghalti kar baitha hai, ghati kar baitha hai dil.
